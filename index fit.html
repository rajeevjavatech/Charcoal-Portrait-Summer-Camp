What You'll Learn
DANCE STYLES

Our camp covers a rich mix of dance forms to make you a well-rounded performer.

01
Zumba Fitness
High-energy Latin-inspired dance fitness. Burn calories while having a blast — the ultimate combination of cardio and rhythm that keeps you coming back.
Fitness • Cardio
02
Hip Hop
Street-born, globally celebrated. Learn foundational grooves, isolations, and freestyling techniques that form the backbone of urban dance culture.
Street • Urban
03
Breakdance
Master the art of B-boying — toprock, footwork, freezes, and power moves. Discipline meets creativity in this foundational hip hop element.
Power • Acrobatic
04
Contemporary Fusion
A blend of modern dance elements woven throughout the camp. Express emotion through movement and develop your unique style and artistic voice.
Expression • Art
